## Typography evidence and decision
Vontier's public identity uses a wide, geometric, technical wordmark and a clean industrial-digital presentation. No redistributable official font package was located. The campaign uses licensed web fonts Rajdhani for angular display typography and IBM Plex Sans for technical body copy, with system fallbacks. This is a documented structural substitute, not a claim that either typeface is official Vontier typography.

## Photography, geometry, interface and motion cues
- Public brand language emphasizes roads, movement, connected mobility, industrial technology, integrated platforms and operational control.
- Geometry: directional cuts, circular mechanical systems, clean lines, high-contrast technical surfaces.
- Motion character: precise, energetic and purposeful rather than playful.
- Original role-derived visual: a torque-converter-like transmission system showing signal intake, controlled coupling, production lock-up and learning return.

## Recommended use by artifact
- Website: full teal/carbon system, animated torque visual, scenario interaction.
- Resume and cover letter: white paper, restrained teal rules, small employer logo, angular section headings.
- Briefs and plan: structured consulting-report layout with teal tables and minimal amber emphasis.

## Independent-candidate distinction
The campaign is clearly labeled as an independent candidate work product, uses nominative company identification, does not imitate official corporate navigation or internal systems, and does not imply endorsement.
