# Vontier Digital Innovation and Artificial Intelligence Leader - Role Intelligence

## Nominal role
Build and lead Vontier's digital innovation and AI strategy, AI Center of Excellence, idea pipeline, rapid pilot portfolio, production deployment, enterprise governance, vendor ecosystem and global scaling across product, operations, IT/OT and commercial teams.

## Actual mandate
Turn fragmented demand across Vontier's strategic business units into a governed enterprise capability that produces customer-facing and operational AI solutions in distributed hardware/software environments - quickly enough to matter and rigorously enough to scale.

## Company moment
- Vontier appointed Rasha Hasaneen Chief Innovation and Growth Officer in February 2026 to drive enterprise innovation, AI strategy and digital modernization.
- Vontier's Connected Mobility strategy spans payments, POS, remote diagnostics, site management, workflow automation, EV charging, telematics, industrial controls, repair diagnostics and multi-energy infrastructure.
- VBS is the explicit enterprise operating system for growth, lean, leadership and continuous improvement.

## Operating tensions
1. Business-unit autonomy vs enterprise leverage.
2. Pilot speed vs production truth.
3. Cloud intelligence vs edge/industrial reality.
4. Outside-in innovation vs VBS operating discipline.
5. Customer value vs platform reuse.

## Decision architecture
Make it easier to decide what enters the portfolio, what gets funded, what remains local, what becomes a common contract, what gets standardized, what gets stopped, what goes to production and what learning is returned to the enterprise.

## White-space thesis
Centralize the evidence and deployment contract, not the customer problem. Business units retain outcome ownership; the enterprise provides reusable architecture, MLOps, security, validation, vendor leverage, adoption mechanisms and transparent portfolio decisions.

## Operating artifact
**Innovation Torque Converter**
- Open: harvest without commitment.
- Coupling: prove under controlled slip.
- Lock-up: transmit value through production architecture and operating ownership.
- Learning return: compound reusable assets through VBS.

It is the combination of hands-on AI workflow design, industrial hardware/software operations, quality systems, distributed launch discipline and the ability to build the operating mechanisms around production AI. The interview should test his live ability to map evidence, authority, architecture, ownership, adoption and reuse for a Vontier scenario.
